A Brief History of the Life Mohammadreza Alimardani

Mohammadreza Alimardani is mostly known by me for his hit-single "Na" that I serendipituously discovered in Haifa, Israel one fateful evening:

  • December 5, 2019: I receive a message from a friend.
  • December 6, 2019: On a random Turkish video, I begin listening to the song.
  • December 7, 2019: I listen to the song again.
  • December 8, 2019: Shit is hot, I begin taking showers with the song in the background.
  • December 9, 2010: Having acknowledged that it is a great song, I add it to my Radio Javan playlist and decide to continue to listen to it more sparingly in the future.
  • December 31, 2020: Still going baby, woo!
